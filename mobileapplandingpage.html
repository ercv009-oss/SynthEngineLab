Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Is there a free trial available?
Yes! You can try our Pro plan free for 14 days with no credit card required. Just download the app and start exploring all premium features.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time from the app settings. No questions asked, and you'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
Does it work offline?
Yes! The app works perfectly offline. All your data is stored locally and will automatically sync when you're back online.
Is my data secure?
Your data security is our top priority. We use end-to-end encryption, and all data is stored securely using industry-standard protocols. We never sell your data to third parties.
How do I contact support?
You can reach our support team 24/7 through the in-app chat, email us at support@applaunch.com, or visit our help center for instant answers.
