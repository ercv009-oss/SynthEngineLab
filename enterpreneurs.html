Business Plan: Interactive Metrics for Entrepreneurs

This page presents a concise business plan and simple CSS‑only interactive charts that let founders review revenue, cost, and profit assumptions.

1. Problem

Entrepreneurs lack quick, visual, low‑tech tools to communicate assumptions and financial projections when pitching or planning.

2. Solution

A lightweight, browser‑native financial snapshot using pure HTML/CSS charts that work even without JavaScript.

3. Market

Solo founders, early‑stage startups, accelerators, and small business owners needing fast, visual planning assets.
